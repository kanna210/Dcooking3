  • 2 steaks
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • BBQ seasoning powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • hurbs, garlic and 1 tbsp butter (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425F
  2. Season the steak with salt, black pepper, and BBQ seasoning powder
  3. Set a pan in high heat with oil
  4. place stakes and fry for 2 minutes for each side
  5. put them in the oven for 2 minutes for rare, 4 minutes for medium, 6 minutes for well done
