Ingredients

  • 151g flour
  • 2g salt
  • 1-2g yeast
  • 4-20g sugar
  • 120g water or 130g milk
  • 5g oil or 8g butter
  • (optional: 10g oil, tomato, herbs, cheese)

Instructions

  2. Add flour and salt to step1.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1 to 3 hours.
  4. put the dough into the mold and wait for 30-45 minutes or until it becomes 1.5 times bigger
