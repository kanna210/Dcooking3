Tortilla Recipe for 10-12 sheets

Ingredients

  • 300g flour
  • 30g flour
  • Flour when you flat them
  • 100g butter
  • 230-240g warm water
  • 5g salt

Instructions

  1. Mix all and add 30g flour.
  2. Wait for 30 minutes.
  3. Flatten thin.
  4. Heat the pan to medium heat.
  5. Cook 1 minute each side.
