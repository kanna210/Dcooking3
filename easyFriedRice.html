Easy Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 100g rice
  • 50g meat
  • 10-20g oil (sesame oil recommended)
  • 2g salt
  • black pepper
  • (optional: chives, corn, green peas, onion)

Instructions

  1. wash the rice and drain the water.
  2. Put rice weight water (100g) plus 110-120g water (so 210-220g water in this case).
  3. Add everything in it and push flash rice.
  4. (optional: You can cook eggs separately to combine them later if you like.)
