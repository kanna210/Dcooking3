Breakfast Burrito [for 2 servings]

Ingredients

  • 4 tortillas (click here for tortilla recipe)
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 strips of bacon
  • Optional: sausage, cheese, hashed potatoes

Instructions

  1. Bake bacon and sausage.
  2. Put them aside and with the bacon oil fry 2 eggs each and put them aside.
  3. Wipe the oil and heat up the tortilla and cheese.
  4. Wrap up the ingredients you want in the pan or on the plate.
