Ingredients [For 1 Serving]

  • 151g flour
  • 2g salt
  • 4g sugar
  • 120g water
  • 2g yeast
  • 5g olive oil or vegetable oil
  • 30-130g cheese (mozzarella and cheddar)
  • Tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Mix water, sugar, and yeast.
  2. Add flour and salt to the mixture.
  3. Let the dough rise for 30 minutes to 3 hours.
  4. Mix the dough and put it in a baking plate. Wait for 15 minutes and then stretch it.
  5. Wait for another 30-60 minutes.
  6. While waiting, preheat the oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).
  7. After waiting, put tomato sauce and half the cheese on the dough.
  8. Bake for 19-23 minutes at 350¬∞F.
  9. Put the remaining cheese on top and bake until it melts (about 1-2 minutes).
