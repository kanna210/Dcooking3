Grilled Recipes

Grilled Potatoes

Ingredients:
- Potatoes
- (Optional: Olive oil and cheese)
- Salt

Steps:
1. Preheat the oven to 425°F and wash potatoes.
2. Coat potatoes with olive oil and salt.
3. Bake potatoes for 45-55 minutes.
4. (Optional) Open the potatoes and add cheese.

Grilled Corn

Ingredients:
- Corns
- (Optional: Butter)
- Salt

Steps:
1. Preheat the oven to 425°F and peel (and maybe wash) corn.
2. Bake corn for 20 minutes.
