Macaroni Cheese [for 2 servings]

Ingredients

  • 226 g uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 42g butter
  • 16g all-purpose flour
  • 3g salt (1/2 teaspoon salt)
  • 366 g milk, any kind
  • 182 g half &half or 90g heavy cream and 90g milk
  • 283 g shredded cheddar cheese
  • (optional: black pepper, chives, bacon, ground beef, onion)

Instructions

  1. boil the macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the sauce: Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Blend in the flour, salt. Cook for 2 minutes. Slowly add the milk and heavy cream, stirring constantly, and cook over medium-low heat for a few minutes until the mixture has thickened like the consistency of gravy, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and add 2/3 shredded cheese, stirring just until melted.
  4. Combine: Add the cooked macaroni noodles and toss to coat. Sprinkle rest of the cheese over the top.
  5. cook with medium-heat until the cheese melts with the lid on in the sauce pan or in the oven
