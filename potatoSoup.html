Potato Soup Recipe

Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 6 strips uncooked bacon (~28g per strip)
  • 43g butter
  • 2 yellow onions (320-400g)
  • 7.5g salt
  • 42g all-purpose flour
  • 1.15kg potatoes
  • 900-945g chicken broth
  • 200-300g milk
  • 155g heavy cream
  • (Optional) Cheese, black pepper, chili pepper, paprika powder

Steps

  1. Fry onions and bacon with butter for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Add flour and mix it. Add chicken broth, and potatoes.
  3. Boil for 10-15 minutes, then add milk, heavy cream, salt, black pepper, and chili pepper.
  4. Simmer for 5 minutes and serve with other toppings.
